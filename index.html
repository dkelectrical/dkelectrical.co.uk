Frequently Asked Questions

What areas do you cover?

We cover Nuneaton, Atherstone, Hinckley, and all surrounding areas in Warwickshire and the East Midlands.

Do you provide free quotes?

Yes, we provide free, no-obligation quotes for all electrical work. Contact us with your requirements and we will get back to you promptly.

Are you qualified and insured?

Yes, DK Electrical is fully certified and insured. We carry out all work to the latest UK electrical standards and regulations (BS 7671).

How quickly can you respond to emergencies?

We offer emergency call-out services. For urgent electrical issues, please call us directly on ... and we will respond as quickly as possible.

What is an EICR and do I need one?

An EICR (Electrical Installation Condition Report) is a formal assessment of the electrical installations in a property. It is recommended for all property owners and is legally required for rentals in England.

Do you offer fixed-price quotes?

Yes, we provide honest, fixed-rate quotes so there are no surprises. The price we quote is the price you pay.
